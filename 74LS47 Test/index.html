Project Description

The exposure unit is controlled by an ATMEGA168 microcontroller. It is interfaced to an PCF8574 I2C bus expander which is interfaced to an HD44780 LCD screen. It is also interfaced to a stop, start and lid trip switches. The microcontroller is powered by a liniar power supply operating at 12VDC and 5VDC. The lights consist of a panel containing 128 UV Leds.
