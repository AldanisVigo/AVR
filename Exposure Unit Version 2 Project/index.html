Project Description

The exposure unit is controlled by an ATMEGA168 microcontroller. It is interfaced to two 74LS47 IC's that drive the counter display which consists of two common anode 7 segment LED didplays. It is also interfaced to a stop, start and lid trip switches. The microcontroller is powered by a liniar power supply operating at 12VDC and 5VDC. The lights consist of a panel containing 128 UV Leds.
